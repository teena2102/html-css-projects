CHEN ZHEYUAN

HIDDEN LOVE

"Hidden Love" is a Chinese romantic drama starring Chen Zheyuan and Zhao Lusi. The story follows Sang Zhi, a young girl who secretly falls for her brother’s best friend, Duan Jiaxu. As she grows up and reunites with him in university, her childhood crush deepens into real love. Duan Jiaxu, initially unaware, eventually returns her feelings with quiet affection. The drama is praised for its soft romance, warm chemistry, and relatable emotions. It explores themes of growing up, first love, and emotional connection. Their bond grows naturally, avoiding unnecessary drama or clichés. “Hidden Love” is loved for its comfort, sweetness, and heartfelt storytelling.

TEENA AGRAWAL

OUR SECRET

"Our Secret" is a Chinese youth romance drama based on the novel Secrets in the Lattice. It follows the story of Ding Xian, a bright and determined girl, and Zhou Siyue, a genius but aloof boy. They meet in high school and slowly develop a deep bond despite their opposite personalities. Their journey captures the sweetness and struggles of young love and academic pressure. As they grow older, their relationship faces long-distance challenges and personal growth. The drama highlights friendship, ambition, and emotional maturity over time. With its nostalgic school setting and heartfelt moments, it touches many hearts. "Our Secret" is a soft, emotional tale of first love and dreams that linger.

LOVE YOU

MR. BAD

"Mr. Bad" is a Chinese fantasy-romance drama starring Shen Yue and Chen Zheyuan. It tells the story of Nan Xing, a romance novelist, who accidentally brings her villainous character, Xiao Wu Di, to life. Though he enters her world as a troublemaker, their relationship soon shifts from chaos to affection. Xiao Wu Di, originally cold and cunning, slowly transforms through love and real-world experiences. Nan Xing learns to face life more boldly, while Wu Di discovers genuine emotions beyond fiction. Their romance blends fantasy and reality in a fun, lighthearted way. With charming chemistry and comedic twists, the story keeps viewers engaged. "Mr. Bad" is a delightful tale of unexpected love and magical second chances.
