Section One

Teri aankhon mein chhupi subah ka noor lagta hai,
Har pal tu paas ho, yeh khwab zaroor lagta hai.
Chupke se tu aaye, jaise khushboo hawa mein,
Dil keh raha ho jaane kyun, tu basa hai dua mein.
Palkein jhuki ho toh kuch keh jaati hain,
Bina bole bhi baatein ho jaati hain.
Tere saath chalti hoon main khud se door,
Jaise likhi hoon main tere hi noor se poori poori.
Ye geet ban gaya hai tujh se milne ke baad,
Teri yaadon mein hi chhupi meri har ek raag.

EXPLORE NATURE

Section Two

In aankhon ki baat hi kuch aur hoti hai,
Yeh chup rehkar bhi mohabbat kar jaati hai.
Ek nazar mein dil ka raaz chura lein,
In jhuki palakon mein sau armaan basa lein.
In labon ki hansi mein ek dard chhupa hai,
Jaise har khushi ka pehlu bhi udaasi se juda hai.
Teri khamoshi bhi ik nagma ban jaaye,
Jo sirf dil hi nahi, rooh tak sun paaye.
Tere aane se mehka hai har mod-e-zindagi,
Teri yaadon mein hi mili hai har bandagi.

LOOK DEEP INTO NATURE

Section Three

Tum de rahi ho dil mein kisi aur ko jagah,
Aur main aaj bhi tere khayalon mein basa hoon.
Tere lamhon mein ab mera zikr bhi nahi,
Par main har dua mein sirf tera naam leta hoon.
Tere chehre pe muskaan kisi aur ke liye hai,
Aur meri aankhon mein aansu sirf tere liye hain.
Jo khwab humne dekhe the saath chalke,
Woh aaj tere kisi aur ke haathon mein pal rahe hain.
Chaha tujhe bepanah, bewajah, beshumaar,
Aur tum chale gaye bina kuch kahe, be-asar.
