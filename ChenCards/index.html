HI,I AM CHEN ZHEYUAN
I AM AN ACTOR
Chen Zheyuan is a talented Chinese actor, singer, and model born on October 29, 1996, in Shenzhen, Guangdong, China.
He graduated from Shenzhen University with a major in Performance.
Chen first gained recognition after participating in the reality show "King of Pop" in 2015.
He made his acting debut in 2017 in the youth drama "All About Secrets."
Chen rose to fame with his charming performance in the hit drama "Hidden Love" (2023) opposite Zhao Lusi.
His character Duan Jiaxu in Hidden Love won many hearts for his calm and sweet demeanor.
He also starred in popular dramas like "Our Secret" and "Mr. Bad," showcasing his versatile acting skills.
Besides acting, Chen Zheyuan is known for his boyish charm, natural screen presence, and gentle personality.
He is admired for keeping a low profile and focusing on his craft rather than gossip or publicity.
With his rising popularity and strong acting potential, Chen is one of the most promising young actors in China today.
